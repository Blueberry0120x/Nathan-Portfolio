About

CIVIL ENGINEER - MUNICIPAL DESIGN

I'm a civil EIT in utility design and land development. Most of my work is water and sewer design and modeling for a light-rail program, plus drainage and entitlement work across California and Washington. I've put together permit packages on the structural and code side — CBC, IRC, NDS-2018, ASCE 7-22 — and I'm in Civil 3D and OpenRoads daily. When production gets repetitive, I write my own tools for it (VBA, AutoLISP, Dynamo). B.S. Civil Engineering; working toward my PE.
